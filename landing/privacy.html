← Back to homepage Privacy & safety

Workflow data should not need to leave your machine.

The analyzer is designed for local/offline workflow audit scenarios. It does not require access to your n8n instance, credentials, or production systems.

Does

  • Reads anonymized workflow JSON exports.
  • Performs static analysis.
  • Counts credential references without exposing values.

Does not

  • Execute workflows.
  • Call the n8n API.
  • Inspect secrets.
  • Provide compliance certification.

Before sharing exports

Remove API keys, tokens, Authorization headers, passwords, customer data, internal hostnames, and production webhook URLs.

Use placeholders such as YOUR_API_KEY, user@example.com, and internal.example.com.

Not affiliated with n8n. Do not send credentials or secrets.
